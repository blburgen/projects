General Information

Ammon is a suburb of Idaho Falls in the eastern part of the state (Wikipedia). It is about 7.61 sq miles in size(Wikipedia). It had a population of 17,694 in 2020(USCB, 2020, Ammon city, Idaho). The median household income of Ammon in 2023 was $76,471 (USCB, 2023, Ammon city, Idaho). This is higher that the state median household income in 2023 which was $74,942 (USCB, 2023, Idaho).

City Hall

Ammon City Hall is located at: 2135 S Ammon Rd, Ammon, ID 83406.
