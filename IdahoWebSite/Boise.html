General Information

Boise is the capital of the State of Idaho. It resides in the southwest part of the state(Visit Idaho). It is a urban city with a size about 85.00 sq miles (Wikipedia). It had a population of 235,684 people in 2020 (USCB, 2020, Boise). Median Household Income for Boise in 2023 was $79,977 (USCB, 2023, Boise). This is higher than the State average at $74,942 (USCB, 2023, Idaho).

City Hall

City Hall is located at: 150 N Capitol Blvd, Boise, ID 83702.
