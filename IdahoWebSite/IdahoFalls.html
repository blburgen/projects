General Information

Idaho Falls is the largest city in the eatern part of the State of Idaho. It is the main city in the Idaho Falls metro, which includes the city of Ammon. It size is about 24.55 sq miles(Wikipedia). Its population in 2020 was 64,818 people (USCB, 2020, Idaho Falls city, Idaho). The median household income in 2023 was $63,049 (USCB, 2023, Idaho Falls city, Idaho). This is less that the state median household income in 2023, which was $74,942 (USCB, 2023, Idaho).
