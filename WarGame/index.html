War Card Game

rules

  1. The player with the higher card wins. Places the cards on the bottom of the winner's deck
    • Ace counts as 1
    • King is the highest card
  2. if there is a tie - war begins
    • 2 cards are removed
    • the second card is used to determine the winner
    • if a tie happens repeat until a winner is determined
